Welcome to **Apoorva**: An accessibility-friendly Hugo theme, ported from the [original Cupper](https://github.com/ThePacielloGroup/cupper) project. Here are some of its features:

{{< ticks >}}
* Screen reader and keyboard accessible
* Responsive
* Numerous shortcodes
* Toggleable dark theme
* Toggleable table of contents per blog post
